Computational Chemistry Workshops - Summer 2016

Electronic Structure - July 18-22, 2016
Molecular Dynamics - July 25-29, 2016

Topics and Exercises

The motivation for both lecture topics and hands-on exercises involves the use of computational tools to model complex biological and chemical problems, such as molecular changes occurring during photosynthesis, or how photons can be efficiently converted to chemical energy.

Topics

  • Electronic Structure Calculations

    • Symmetry Concepts and Group Theory
    • Hartree-Fock and Density Functional Methods
    • Reaction Pathways and Transition States
    • Spectroscopy of Organic and Transition Metal Compounds

  • Molecular Dynamics Simulations

    • Statistical Thermodynamics
    • The Molecular Mechanics Force Field
    • Introduction to Molecular Dynamics
